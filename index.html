I'm Here to Listen

We all go through tough times, and sometimes it helps just to have someone who listens.

Why Am I Doing This?

I’ve been through anxiety myself and know how much it helps when someone simply listens.

I enjoy connecting with others and would like to offer my time to be that supportive listener for you.

Is There a Cost for This?

No, this is completely free. I’m here to help.

What About My Identity?

Your identity is safe with me. You don’t need to share your true identity unless you want to. Feel free to speak openly.

Who Am I?

I am not a certified therapist or even a therapist. I’m simply someone who likes to talk to people and help them wherever possible.

If you need someone to talk to, I'm here for you.
